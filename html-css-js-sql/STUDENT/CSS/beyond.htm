Books & Beyond

About us... Have you ever wanted to spend some quiet time away from home? Many of us find that solitude buried in the pages of a good book or lost in the sounds of musical instruments. Maybe you want to visit with a friend over a cup of cappuccino or a fresh-squeezed juice blend, or just take a break from the shopping mall and treat yourself to a scrumptious dessert. You can do all those things at Books & Beyond.

Some people say it is a bookstore. Some call it a music shop, while others think of it as a great place to meet and have refreshments. Books & Beyond has become a unique phenomenon that not only offers this area’s largest selection of sale books but has also branched into the world of music, with an inventory of CDs and tapes ranging from New Age to Alternative Rock and everything in between.

What makes us different is our atmosphere. We encourage our visitors to relax with a book in one of our quiet rooms, or take some reading material into our Coffee and Juice Bar to read while snacking.
